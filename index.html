This is Pawcraft

A cosy furry gaming community sponsored by BoltWolf Networks.

Meet the team

Pawcraft has a friendly, diverse team led by the Project Manager, Yuki. The team has a lot of skills in moderation, design and systems administration. They love to help everyone out.

The Team

Come play some games with us!

Pawcraft is focused on making fun, safe furry communities on different video games. Pawcraft was initially a server specialising in modded Minecraft, but is now expanding to other games such as Garry's Mod and Starbound. The list is growing, come check out some of our servers!

Join a server

Come chat with us!

Pawcraft has a very cosy Discord server where most discussion takes place. It has a lot of channels and all discussion within the rules is welcome. You can also find help from some of the moderators there, or talk to the Project Manager.

Current projects

Pawcraft not only specialises in gaming servers, but also in other gaming related projects. Here are some examples!

Pawcraft Pack

Formerly named Project Pawcraft, this is our first, biggest, most developed project is the Pawcraft modpack for Minecraft. It includes many mods, and lots of textures and exclusive furry items. Started in February 2015, the modpack has grown rapidly in size and is initially what created this community.
